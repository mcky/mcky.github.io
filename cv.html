About me

I'm a London based developer, specialising in front-end, with full-stack, product and hardware experience. I've got a keen interest in UX, rapid prototyping and creative uses of technology.

I strive to write clean, maintainable and performant code across a wide range of sectors and technologies. My preferred technologies are React, Node and scss, but I've got experience integrating with Rails/Django apps and prototyping Swift, Arduino and Python.

Available for short to mid term contracts, drop me an email ross@rossmackay.co or tweet me @r_mcky if you have a project you think is a good fit.
